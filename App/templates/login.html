We are focused on reducing food wastage

It was discovered that for such a small country, Trinidad and Tobago (as well as Caribbean countries and Latin America combined), we waste about 50 million tons of food annually. This results in, not only an environmental problem but an economical one too. With FoodRefinedTT, we look to lower the amount of food disposed.
